Swatch card — anatomy

  • 1Iridescent rim (#9DB8FF→#FFB7C9) marks the active / hero card only — never decoration. A slow gloss sweep is the "dewy" tap moment.
  • 2Glass status badge floats on the smear so expiry reads without fighting the shade.
  • 3Bottom scrim guarantees white text is legible over any shade — this is how C survives a 87-blush wall.
  • 4Shade code, set huge in Spline Sans Mono — the identity, the thing she reads at arm's length in the aisle.

Notes for build. Tokens live in hifi/seoul-glass.css (e.g. --sg-glass, --sg-iri, --sg-fresh). The smear is data-driven: each item needs a --s1 / --s2 / --sdeep triplet — a hex column the Sheet doesn't have yet (flagged). Card C text legibility is solved by the scrim, not per-pixel contrast math, so it works no-build. Reduced-motion drops the gloss sweep and shows the end state. Tab bar carries 5 slots (Catalog included) per your pick — Scan raised at center.
